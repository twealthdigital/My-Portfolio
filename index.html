My Professional Journey

I began my career in enterprise IT operations, where I managed complex systems for mid-sized companies across different industries. What I observed was striking: businesses were losing millions to manual data entry, disconnected CRM platforms, missed follow-up opportunities, and inefficient workflows that nobody had time to fix. That frustration became the catalyst for my mission: to build systems that eliminate operational waste and drive predictable, scalable growth.

Over the past 7+ years, I've transitioned from traditional IT operations to specialized hyper-automation architecture. I've worked with companies ranging from bootstrap startups to established enterprises, helping them leverage technology not just to keep up, but to pull ahead of competitors. My approach combines technical depth with business pragmatism — I don't build automation for the sake of technology; I build it to solve real problems and deliver measurable ROI.

What I Actually Do (Day to Day)

I design and implement complete automation ecosystems. This isn't just connecting Zapier to Gmail — that's table stakes. I build full-stack automation solutions that include:

  • CRM Architecture: Designing and implementing CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, GoHighLevel) that actually match your sales process.
  • AI Workflow Integration: Incorporating AI where it adds value — lead scoring, content generation, data extraction, customer support automation.
  • Email Marketing Systems: Building sequences that nurture leads, recover abandoned carts, and drive repeat purchases.
  • Data Enrichment Pipelines: Creating automated systems that enrich your lead data from Apollo.io, Clearbit, and Hunter.io.
  • Cross-Platform Integration: Connecting your entire tech stack so data flows seamlessly between all your tools.
